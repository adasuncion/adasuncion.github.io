Program Objective:

The program prepares students with the practical skills in multimedia technology, web/mobile development, and network/system administration to manage an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a startup business or pursue a teaching career.

Specifically, the students of the program should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or processes by applying knowledge in computer or network systems, and development or productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the degree program.
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunities.
